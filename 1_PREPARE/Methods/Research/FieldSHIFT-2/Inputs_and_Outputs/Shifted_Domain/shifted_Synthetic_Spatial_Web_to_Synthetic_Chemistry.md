The Spatial Web represents a paradigm shift in how we interact with our environment, merging physical and digital realms to create seamless experiences. Its core principles include:

### 2. Examination of Domain B: Chemistry

Chemistry is fundamentally concerned with the composition, structure, properties, and changes of matter. Its core concepts include:

- **Chemical Reactions**: Processes in which substances transform into different substances through the breaking and forming of bonds.
- **Catalysis**: The acceleration of chemical reactions by catalysts, which are not consumed in the process.
- **Chemical Equilibrium**: A state where the rates of forward and reverse reactions are equal, leading to stable concentrations of reactants and products.
- **Reaction Kinetics**: The study of reaction rates and the factors influencing them, such as concentration, temperature, and pressure.
- **Thermodynamics**: The study of energy changes in chemical reactions, determining spontaneity and equilibrium states.

Current challenges in chemistry include the need for sustainable practices, the development of efficient catalysts, and the exploration of complex reaction mechanisms.
