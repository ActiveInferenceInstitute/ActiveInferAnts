**Core Principles:**

**Key Concepts:**
- **Supply Chain Management:** Integration of all activities involved in sourcing, procurement, and logistics.
- **Last-Mile Delivery:** The final step of the supply chain, ensuring products reach customers efficiently.
- **Sustainable Logistics:** Practices focused on reducing environmental impact and promoting corporate social responsibility.

**Historical Development:**
Logistics has evolved from military supply chains to a critical component of global commerce, driven by technological advancements and globalization.

**Current Trends:**
Emerging trends include digital transformation, the rise of e-commerce, autonomous delivery vehicles, and sustainability initiatives.

**Unique Perspectives:**
Logistics emphasizes collaboration among stakeholders, data-driven decision-making, and adaptability to changing market conditions.
