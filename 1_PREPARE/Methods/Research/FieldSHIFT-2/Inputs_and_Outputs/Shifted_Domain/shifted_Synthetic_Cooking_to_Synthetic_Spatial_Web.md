## Domain Shift: From Cuisine to the Spatial Web

### 1. Analysis of Domain A: Cuisine

**Core Principles:**
- **Culinary Techniques:** The methods of preparing food, including techniques such as braising, sautéing, and fermentation.
- **Flavor Profiles:** The combination of tastes and aromas that define dishes, influenced by cultural and regional ingredients.
- **Ingredient Interactions:** The chemical and physical interactions between ingredients during cooking, which alter textures, flavors, and nutritional content.
- **Cultural Context:** Cuisine is deeply rooted in cultural traditions, environmental factors, and historical developments.

**Methodologies:**
- **Recipe Development:** Systematic testing and refinement of dishes to achieve desired flavors and textures.
- **Mise en Place:** The organization and preparation of ingredients before cooking to ensure efficiency.
- **Culinary Fusion:** The blending of different culinary traditions to create innovative dishes.

**Key Concepts:**
- **Seasonality:** The importance of using ingredients at their peak for optimal flavor and nutrition.
- **Fermentation:** A process that transforms ingredients, enhancing flavors and extending shelf life.
- **Flavor Pairing:** The science of combining ingredients based on shared flavor compounds.

**Historical Development:**
- The evolution of cuisine has been influenced by trade, migration, and technological advancements in cooking methods.

**Current Trends:**
- The rise of plant-based diets, molecular gastronomy, and sustainable cooking practices.
