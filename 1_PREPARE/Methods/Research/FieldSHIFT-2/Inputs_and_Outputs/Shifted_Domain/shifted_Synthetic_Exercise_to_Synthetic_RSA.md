### 2. Thorough Examination of Domain B: RSA Cryptography

**Current Paradigms and Challenges in RSA:**
- **Public-Key Infrastructure:** RSA relies on key pairs (public and private) for secure communication, but key management remains a challenge.
- **Mathematical Foundations:** The security of RSA is based on the difficulty of factoring large prime numbers, which is computationally intensive.
- **Vulnerabilities:** RSA is susceptible to various attacks, including timing attacks, chosen ciphertext attacks, and issues related to key generation and management.
- **Performance Limitations:** RSA is slower than symmetric algorithms, making it less suitable for encrypting large volumes of data directly.
