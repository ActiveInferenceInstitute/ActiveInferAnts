## 1. Introduction
- **Summary of the Dissertation**: This dissertation explores the intersection of neural networks and the Free Energy Principle (FEP), aiming to develop a unified framework that enhances our understanding of cognitive processes through predictive coding and active inference. The research questions focus on the applicability of neural network principles to the FEP, the role of synaptic plasticity in updating generative models, and the optimization of hierarchical structures for cognitive tasks.
