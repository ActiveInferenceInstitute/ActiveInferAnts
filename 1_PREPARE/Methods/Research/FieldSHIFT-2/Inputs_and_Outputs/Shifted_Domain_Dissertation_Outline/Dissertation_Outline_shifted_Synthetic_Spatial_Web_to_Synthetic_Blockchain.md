# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transposing the Spatial Web to Blockchain

## Executive Summary
This dissertation explores the innovative integration of the Spatial Web and blockchain technology, creating a novel framework termed the **Spatial Blockchain**. By examining the synergies between the immersive, interactive capabilities of the Spatial Web and the decentralized, secure nature of blockchain, this research aims to develop new applications that enhance user experiences and improve data management across various sectors. The potential impact of this research is profound, offering transformative solutions for industries such as logistics, urban planning, and environmental management.

## Introduction
### Background of the Shifted Domain
The Spatial Web merges the physical and digital realms through technologies like Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR), while blockchain provides a decentralized ledger for secure transactions. This dissertation investigates how these two domains can be integrated to create a Spatial Blockchain, enhancing user interaction and data integrity.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
The significance of this research lies in its potential to revolutionize how we manage and interact with data in both digital and physical environments. By creating a Spatial Blockchain, we can leverage the strengths of both domains to address existing challenges in data management, security, and user engagement.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
1. How can principles of the Spatial Web be effectively integrated into blockchain technology?
2. What are the implications of this integration for industries such as logistics, real estate, and urban planning?
3. What new theoretical constructs emerge from this interdisciplinary fusion?
