# PhD Dissertation Plan: Fusion of Quantum Computation and Guest Experience

## Executive Summary
This dissertation aims to explore the innovative integration of principles from quantum computation into the guest experience within the hospitality industry. By leveraging concepts such as superposition, entanglement, and quantum gates, this research will propose a novel framework for enhancing personalization, interconnected service delivery, and dynamic guest interactions. The significance of this study lies in its potential to revolutionize hospitality practices, improve guest satisfaction, and set new standards for operational efficiency. The outcomes could lead to the establishment of a new sub-field, "Quantum Hospitality," and provide a roadmap for future interdisciplinary research and applications.

## Introduction
### Background of the Shifted Domain
Quantum computation represents a paradigm shift in information processing, utilizing the principles of quantum mechanics to perform calculations that classical computers cannot. Conversely, the guest experience in hospitality focuses on creating memorable interactions and personalized services for guests. This dissertation will explore the intersection of these two domains, identifying how quantum principles can enhance the guest experience.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
The proposed research is significant as it challenges traditional notions of both quantum computation and hospitality. By applying quantum principles to guest experience, this study will introduce a new lens through which to view service delivery, potentially transforming both fields. The novelty lies in the creation of a theoretical framework that merges these disparate domains, fostering innovative practices in hospitality management.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
1. How can quantum principles be applied to enhance personalization in guest experiences?
2. In what ways can interconnected service delivery improve overall guest satisfaction?
3. What are the implications of adopting a quantum-inspired approach for hospitality operations?
