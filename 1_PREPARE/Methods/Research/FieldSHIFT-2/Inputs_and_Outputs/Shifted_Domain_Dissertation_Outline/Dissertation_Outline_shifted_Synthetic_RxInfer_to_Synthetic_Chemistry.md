# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transposing Concepts from RxInfer.jl (Probabilistic Programming and Bayesian Inference) into Chemistry

## Executive Summary
This dissertation explores the innovative integration of probabilistic programming principles, specifically those from RxInfer.jl, into the field of chemistry. By applying probabilistic modeling and Bayesian inference to chemical systems, the research aims to address significant challenges in reaction modeling, data uncertainty, and dynamic system behaviors. The anticipated outcomes include the development of new modeling frameworks, enhanced predictive capabilities in chemical reactions, and a transformative impact on both theoretical and practical aspects of chemistry.
