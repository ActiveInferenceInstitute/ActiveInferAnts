# Executive Summary

This dissertation proposes a groundbreaking exploration of the Shifted Domain, which integrates core principles of health care with innovative strategies aimed at enhancing patient experience. By synthesizing patient-centered care, quality service delivery, and holistic engagement, this research aims to develop a comprehensive framework that redefines patient experience in health care settings. This fusion not only addresses existing gaps in both fields but also sets the stage for novel methodologies and practical applications that can significantly improve patient outcomes, satisfaction, and overall health system efficiency.

# Introduction

## Background of the Shifted Domain

The Shifted Domain represents an innovative convergence of health care principles and patient experience strategies, emphasizing the need for a more integrated approach. As health care continues to evolve, there is an increasing recognition of the importance of patient engagement and satisfaction as critical components of effective care.

## Significance and Novelty of the Research

This research is significant as it addresses the pressing need for a paradigm shift in health care delivery, focusing on the patient experience as a central tenet. The novelty lies in the application of health care methodologies to enhance patient experience, creating a dual impact that benefits both patients and health care providers.

## Overarching Research Questions and Objectives

1. How can principles of patient-centered care be effectively integrated into enhancing patient experience?
2. What role does technology play in improving patient engagement and satisfaction?
3. In what ways can holistic approaches to health care enhance overall patient outcomes?
