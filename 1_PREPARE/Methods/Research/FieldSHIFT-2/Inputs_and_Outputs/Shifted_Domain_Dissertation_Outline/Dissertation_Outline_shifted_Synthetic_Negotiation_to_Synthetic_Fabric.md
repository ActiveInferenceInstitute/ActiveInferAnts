# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transposing Negotiation Principles into the Fabric Industry and Arts

## Executive Summary
This dissertation aims to explore the transformative potential of applying negotiation principles from the field of negotiation (Domain A) to the fabric industry and arts (Domain B). By investigating how negotiation strategies can enhance collaboration, creativity, and sustainability in the fabric industry, this research will address existing challenges while fostering innovative practices. The significance of this work lies in its potential to reshape the fabric industry, promote cultural sensitivity, and establish a framework for interdisciplinary collaboration. The dissertation will provide a comprehensive roadmap for doctoral candidates to contribute meaningfully to this emerging field.

## Introduction

### Background of the Shifted Domain
The fabric industry has long been characterized by a complex interplay of design, production, and cultural heritage. However, the rapid pace of change in consumer preferences, sustainability concerns, and the rise of fast fashion have created significant challenges. Simultaneously, negotiation has evolved as a critical skill set in various fields, emphasizing collaboration and mutual benefit. This dissertation proposes a novel integration of these two domains, focusing on how negotiation can serve as a catalyst for innovation in the fabric industry.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
This research is novel in its approach to merging negotiation principles with fabric design and production processes. By addressing the gaps in collaboration and cultural sensitivity within the industry, this dissertation will contribute to both academic literature and practical applications. The significance of this work extends beyond the fabric industry, offering insights applicable to other creative fields facing similar challenges.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
1. How can negotiation strategies enhance collaboration among stakeholders in the fabric industry?
2. What role does cultural sensitivity play in the negotiation process within fabric design?
3. How can sustainable practices be integrated into negotiation frameworks in the fabric industry?
4. What innovative practices can emerge from applying negotiation principles to the fabric industry?
