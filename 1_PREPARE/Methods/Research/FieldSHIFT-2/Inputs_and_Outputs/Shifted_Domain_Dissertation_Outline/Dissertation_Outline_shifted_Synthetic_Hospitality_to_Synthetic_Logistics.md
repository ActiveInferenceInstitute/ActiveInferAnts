# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transforming Logistics through Guest Experience Principles

## Executive Summary
This dissertation aims to explore the transformative potential of integrating guest experience principles from the hospitality industry into the logistics sector. By examining the synergies between these two domains, the research seeks to develop innovative strategies that enhance customer satisfaction, emotional engagement, and operational efficiency in logistics. The findings are expected to contribute significantly to the field of logistics, offering a new perspective on customer interaction and service delivery that prioritizes personalization and sustainability.

## Introduction

### Background of the Shifted Domain
The Shifted Domain represents an innovative fusion of guest experience principles from hospitality with the operational frameworks of logistics. This intersection creates opportunities to rethink logistics as a customer-centric service that values emotional connections and personalized experiences.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
The research is significant as it challenges traditional logistics paradigms that prioritize efficiency over customer satisfaction. By applying hospitality principles, the study aims to redefine logistics as an experience-driven industry, thereby enhancing customer loyalty and operational effectiveness.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
- How can guest experience principles be effectively integrated into logistics operations?
- What are the potential impacts of personalized logistics services on customer satisfaction?
- How can emotional engagement strategies transform the logistics customer journey?
