# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transposing Negotiation to Guest Experience in Hospitality

## Executive Summary

This dissertation aims to explore the innovative intersection of negotiation principles and guest experience within the hospitality industry. By transposing core negotiation strategies—such as effective communication, empathy, and emotional intelligence—into the realm of guest interactions, this research seeks to redefine how hospitality businesses engage with their guests. The significance of this study lies in its potential to enhance guest satisfaction, foster loyalty, and improve service quality through a structured approach to training and operational practices. The findings will not only contribute to the academic literature but also offer practical applications for industry stakeholders, thereby impacting the broader field of hospitality management.

## Introduction

### Background of the Shifted Domain

The hospitality industry is undergoing a transformation driven by evolving guest expectations, technological advancements, and the need for personalized experiences. Concurrently, negotiation has emerged as a critical skill in various sectors, emphasizing the importance of communication, empathy, and adaptability. This dissertation proposes a novel framework that integrates negotiation principles into guest experience management, creating a synergistic approach that enhances both guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research

The fusion of negotiation strategies with guest experience management is a groundbreaking approach that has not been extensively explored in existing literature. This research fills a critical gap by providing a theoretical and practical framework for hospitality professionals to leverage negotiation techniques to improve service delivery. The novelty of this research lies in its interdisciplinary nature, merging insights from negotiation theory, psychology, and hospitality management.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives

1. How can negotiation principles be effectively integrated into guest experience management?
2. What impact does training hospitality staff in negotiation techniques have on guest satisfaction and loyalty?
3. How can the application of negotiation strategies enhance service recovery processes in hospitality?

## Methodology

### Research Design Overview

A mixed-methods research design will be employed, combining quantitative surveys and qualitative interviews to gather comprehensive data on the impact of negotiation training on guest experience.

### Data Collection Methods

- **Surveys**: Administering pre- and post-training surveys to hospitality staff and guests to measure satisfaction and service quality.
- **Interviews**: Conducting in-depth interviews with industry professionals to gather insights on the application of negotiation techniques.

### Analytical Approaches

- **Quantitative Analysis**: Statistical analysis of survey data using regression models to assess the impact of negotiation training on guest satisfaction.
- **Qualitative Analysis**: Thematic analysis of interview transcripts to identify common themes and insights related to the integration of negotiation principles.

### Ethical Considerations

Ensuring informed consent, confidentiality, and the ethical treatment of participants throughout the research process.
