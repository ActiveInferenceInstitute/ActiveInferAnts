# PhD Dissertation Plan: Game Design Principles Applied to Mitochondrial Biology

## Executive Summary
This dissertation explores the innovative intersection of game design principles and mitochondrial biology, proposing a transformative framework that enhances our understanding of cellular processes and educational methodologies. By leveraging concepts such as player engagement, iterative development, and feedback loops, this research aims to create a novel lens through which mitochondrial function can be analyzed and taught. The potential impact includes advancements in metabolic disease research, educational reforms in biological sciences, and the establishment of new interdisciplinary collaborations.

## Introduction
### Background of the Shifted Domain
The integration of game design principles into mitochondrial biology represents a groundbreaking shift in how we approach both fields. Game design, traditionally focused on user engagement and experience, offers unique insights into the dynamic and interactive nature of biological systems. Mitochondria, as essential cellular organelles, are integral to energy production and metabolism, making them a prime candidate for this innovative exploration.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
This research is significant as it proposes a novel framework that transcends traditional boundaries between disciplines. By applying game design principles to mitochondrial biology, we can foster a deeper understanding of cellular functions, enhance educational practices, and develop innovative therapeutic strategies for metabolic disorders. The novelty lies in the systematic transposition of game mechanics to biological processes, offering fresh perspectives on energy regulation and cellular signaling.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
- How can game design principles be effectively integrated into the study of mitochondrial biology?
- What are the implications of this integration for understanding mitochondrial function and dysfunction?
- How can this interdisciplinary approach enhance educational practices in biological sciences?
- What novel hypotheses and models can emerge from this intersection?
