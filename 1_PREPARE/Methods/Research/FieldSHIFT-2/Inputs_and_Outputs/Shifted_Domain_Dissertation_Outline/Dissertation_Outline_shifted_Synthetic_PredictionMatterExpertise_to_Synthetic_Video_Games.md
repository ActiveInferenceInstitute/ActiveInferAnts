# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transposing Prediction Matter Expertise (PME) into Game Design

## Executive Summary
This dissertation aims to explore the innovative integration of Prediction Matter Expertise (PME) into game design, creating a Shifted Domain that enhances player engagement and satisfaction through predictive analytics and adaptive systems. The research will investigate the transformative potential of this fusion, proposing new methodologies and frameworks that can revolutionize the game design process. By leveraging interdisciplinary approaches and empirical studies, this dissertation seeks to establish a comprehensive understanding of how predictive modeling can reshape player experiences, ultimately contributing to both academic discourse and practical applications in the gaming industry.

## Introduction
### Background of the Shifted Domain
The convergence of PME and game design represents an unprecedented opportunity to enhance player experiences through data-driven insights and adaptive mechanics. PME emphasizes the synthesis of knowledge across disciplines to make informed predictions, while game design focuses on creating engaging interactive experiences. This dissertation will explore how these domains can inform and enrich each other.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
The significance of this research lies in its potential to address existing challenges in game design, such as player engagement and retention, through the application of predictive analytics. The novelty of this approach is in its systematic transposition of PME principles into game design, creating a framework that can be utilized by designers to craft more personalized and engaging experiences.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
1. How can predictive analytics be effectively integrated into game design to enhance player engagement?
2. What methodologies can be developed to facilitate the application of PME principles in game design?
3. What are the implications of this integration for the future of game design and player experiences?
