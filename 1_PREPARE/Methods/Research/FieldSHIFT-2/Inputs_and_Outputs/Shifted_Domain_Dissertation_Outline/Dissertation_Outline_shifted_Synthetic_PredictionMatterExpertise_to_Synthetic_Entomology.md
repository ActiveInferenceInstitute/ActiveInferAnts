# PhD Dissertation Plan: Predictive Entomology - Integrating Prediction Matter Expertise into Entomology

## Executive Summary
This dissertation proposes a novel interdisciplinary framework termed "Predictive Entomology," which integrates principles and methodologies from Prediction Matter Expertise (PME) into the field of Entomology. The scope encompasses the development of predictive models and analytical tools to address ecological challenges related to insect populations and their behaviors. This research holds significant potential for advancing both theoretical and practical applications, including improved pest management strategies, enhanced conservation efforts, and a deeper understanding of ecosystem dynamics. The impact of this work could revolutionize how entomologists approach research, leading to more sustainable practices and informed decision-making in environmental policy and agriculture.
