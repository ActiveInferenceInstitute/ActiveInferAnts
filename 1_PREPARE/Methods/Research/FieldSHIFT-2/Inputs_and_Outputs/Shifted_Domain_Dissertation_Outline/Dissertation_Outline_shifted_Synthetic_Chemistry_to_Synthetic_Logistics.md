# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transposing Chemistry to Logistics

## Executive Summary
This dissertation explores the innovative integration of principles from the field of chemistry into logistics, proposing a transformative framework that enhances efficiency, sustainability, and optimization in supply chain management. By analyzing the isomorphisms between chemical processes and logistical operations, this research aims to establish a new paradigm for understanding and improving logistics through chemical analogies. The potential impact of this work extends beyond logistics, influencing related fields and contributing to broader societal challenges such as sustainability and resource management.
