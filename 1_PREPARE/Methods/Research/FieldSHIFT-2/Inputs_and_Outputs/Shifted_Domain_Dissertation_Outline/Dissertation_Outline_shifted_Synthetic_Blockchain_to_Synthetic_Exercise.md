# PhD Dissertation Plan: Integrating Blockchain Principles into Exercise Science

## Executive Summary
This dissertation proposes a groundbreaking integration of blockchain technology into the field of exercise science, creating a Shifted Domain that enhances accountability, motivation, and personalization in fitness. By leveraging blockchain principles such as decentralization, transparency, and smart contracts, this research aims to revolutionize how individuals engage with exercise, track their progress, and achieve their fitness goals. The significance of this work lies in its potential to not only transform individual fitness experiences but also to influence broader public health initiatives and create new economic models in the fitness industry.

## Introduction
### Background of the Shifted Domain
The fusion of blockchain technology and exercise science represents an innovative approach to addressing longstanding challenges in fitness engagement and adherence. As exercise science increasingly incorporates technology, the application of decentralized systems offers new solutions to existing problems.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
This research is significant as it explores uncharted territory by applying blockchain principles to exercise science. It aims to create a more transparent, accountable, and engaging fitness ecosystem, which can lead to enhanced health outcomes and potentially reshape the fitness industry.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
1. How can blockchain technology enhance accountability and adherence in exercise programs?
2. What are the implications of smart contracts for personalized training regimens?
3. How can tokenization of exercise achievements motivate individuals to engage in physical activity?

## Literature Review
### Historical Context of the Original Domains
- **Blockchain:** Originating with Bitcoin, blockchain has evolved through various applications, highlighting its potential beyond cryptocurrencies.
- **Exercise Science:** Traditionally focused on physical activity for health and performance, exercise science has increasingly incorporated technology to enhance training and monitoring.

### Current State of Knowledge in Both Fields
- Blockchain is currently being integrated with AI and IoT, but its application in health and fitness remains limited.
- Exercise science has seen technological advancements, yet challenges in adherence and personalized training persist.

### Gaps and Opportunities Presented by the Shifted Domain
The intersection of these two fields reveals significant gaps, particularly in how decentralized technologies can address challenges in exercise adherence, data management, and personalized training.

## Theoretical Framework
### Foundational Theories from Original Domains
- **Blockchain Theories:** Decentralization, transparency, and trust in digital transactions.
- **Exercise Theories:** Behavior change models and adherence theories in physical activity.

### New Theoretical Constructs Emerging from the Shift
