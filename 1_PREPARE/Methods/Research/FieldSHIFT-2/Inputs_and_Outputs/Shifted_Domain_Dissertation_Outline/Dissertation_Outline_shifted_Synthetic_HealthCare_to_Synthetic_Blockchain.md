# PhD Dissertation Plan: Transforming Health Care through Blockchain Technology

## Executive Summary
This dissertation aims to explore the transformative potential of integrating blockchain technology into the health care sector. By examining the underlying principles of both domains, the research will identify synergies that can enhance patient experiences, improve data security, and redefine health care delivery. The dissertation will provide a comprehensive framework for understanding how blockchain can address existing challenges in health care, offering innovative solutions that promote patient-centered care and operational efficiency. The potential impact of this research extends beyond academic contributions, promising significant advancements in industry practices, policy formulation, and societal health outcomes.

## Introduction
### Background of the Shifted Domain
The intersection of health care and blockchain technology represents a Shifted Domain characterized by the convergence of patient-centered care principles with decentralized, secure data management solutions. This fusion aims to address critical challenges in health care, such as data privacy, interoperability, and patient engagement.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
This research is significant as it presents a novel approach to improving health care delivery through blockchain technology. It challenges traditional paradigms by proposing that decentralized systems can enhance the quality and accessibility of patient care, thereby revolutionizing the health care landscape.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
- How can blockchain technology enhance patient data management and security in health care?
- What are the implications of smart contracts for automating patient consent and care protocols?
- In what ways can the integration of blockchain redefine patient experiences and health care outcomes?
