# PhD Dissertation Plan: From Cuisine to the Spatial Web

## Executive Summary
This dissertation proposes an innovative exploration of the Shifted Domain that merges the culinary arts with the Spatial Web. By drawing parallels between culinary techniques and digital design principles, this research aims to redefine user experiences in digital environments. The significance of this work lies in its potential to enhance user engagement, streamline data processing, and foster cultural relevance in digital interactions. The outcomes of this dissertation will contribute to both the culinary and technological fields, paving the way for novel interdisciplinary collaborations and practical applications.

## Introduction

### Background of the Shifted Domain
The Shifted Domain represents a groundbreaking fusion of Cuisine and the Spatial Web, where culinary principles inform the design and functionality of digital spaces. This domain shift reflects the growing need for more immersive and culturally relevant user experiences in an increasingly digital world.

### Significance and Novelty of the Research
This research is significant as it challenges traditional boundaries between disciplines, offering a fresh perspective on user experience design. By applying culinary concepts to the Spatial Web, we can create richer, more engaging digital environments that resonate with users on a personal and cultural level.

### Overarching Research Questions and Objectives
1. How can culinary techniques enhance user experience design in the Spatial Web?
2. What parallels exist between culinary practices and data processing methods?
3. How can cultural context influence user engagement in digital spaces?

- **Cuisine**: Trends in plant-based diets, molecular gastronomy, and sustainable practices.

## Methodology

### Research Design Overview
This research will employ a mixed-methods approach, combining qualitative and quantitative data to explore the integration of culinary principles into the Spatial Web.

### Data Collection Methods
- Surveys and interviews with culinary experts and digital designers.
- User experience testing of prototypes inspired by culinary techniques.

### Analytical Approaches
- Statistical analysis of user engagement metrics.
- Thematic analysis of qualitative data from interviews.

### Ethical Considerations
Ensuring informed consent from participants, maintaining data privacy, and addressing any potential biases in research design.
